Doughnuts

This is a great doughnut, it's light, fluffy and delicious.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1-1/3 cup milk
  • 2-1/2 tsp (1 pck) instant yeast
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp vainilla extract

Glaze:

  • 4 tsp butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Warm milk with 1 tbsp sugar, add yeast, and mix
  2. Mix with a dough hook with the rest of sugar, melted butter, eggs, flour and vainilla
  3. Knead until the dough is soft and smooth buy still slightly sticky
  4. Let dough rise until it doubles
  5. Roll the dough into a half inch thickness sheet
  6. Cut into doughnuts
  7. Line them up in a baking tray and rise for 45 min
  8. Bake at 350Â°F for 10 min
  9. Let them cool for 10 min
  10. Mix the glaze ingredients until smooth
  11. Glaze the doughnuts and let the dry
  12. Enjoy!
