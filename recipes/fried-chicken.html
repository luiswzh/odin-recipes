Fried Chicken

This is a great fried chicken, it's nicely seasoned, juicy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 whole chicken cutted into parts
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp grounded black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cups self rising flour
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Season chicken pieces with pepper (1 tsp), garlic, and onion powder
  2. In a large bowl mix eggs, water, and buffalo sauce
  3. Preheat oil in a deep fryer to 350Â°F
  4. In another bowl mix flour and remaining pepper
  5. Dip each piece of chicken in egg mixture then in flour mix
  6. Fry the chicken for about 10 min each piece
  7. Place chicken in a wire rack and let it cool for 10 min
  8. Enjoy!
