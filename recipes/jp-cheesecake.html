Japanese Cheesecake

This is a great japanese cheesecake, it's light, fluffy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup (30g) cake flour
  • 2 tbsp (20g) cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup (180g) cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp (30g) unsalted butter
  • 3-1/2 tbsp (50g) milk
  • 3 large egg yolk
  • 1/2 tsp (2g) vainilla extract
  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/3 cup (70g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp (2g) lemon juice
  • powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F
  2. Line the bottom of a 6 inch round pan
  3. Melt butter and cream cheese on a saucepan, stir until it reaches 130°F
  4. Add egg yolk and vainilla
  5. Sift in flour and cornstarch and mix
  6. Make meringue in another bowl
  7. Mix a third of the meringue in the mixture
  8. Pour the mixture into the rest of the meringue and mix
  9. Pour into pan
  10. Put the pan on a large deep baking tray and fill with water
  11. Bake for 30 min at 300°F then bake for another 30 min at 230°F
  12. Finally increase temperature to 275°F and bake for 13-15 minutes
  13. Cool down for 15 minutes and decorate with powdered sugar
  14. Enjoy!
